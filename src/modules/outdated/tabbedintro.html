Welcome to Really Bad Players

We are a guild with a rich history, spanning back to the Burning Crusade expansion. Over the years the guild has undergone many cycles and evolution; many hardships and challenges; but we have continued to grow and excel. Founded on the low-pop, backwater server of US-Terenas, we overcame many recruiting challenges, but excelled and always cleared content in a timely manner.

As subscriber counts dwindled and recruiting became an even bigger obstacle, we found ourselves moving to US-Blackrock late in the Cataclysm expansion. We came to Blackrock as an "unheard of" guild, but in a single, short expansion, we cemented ourselves as the server's #3 progression guild and top 2-night guild.

But a raiding guild is only as strong as its weakest link, so we continue to recruit, continue to evolve, and continue to excel. We believe in the motto: "If you're not recruiting, you're dying." So we ask you this:

Do you have what it takes?

RAID TIMES

MANDATORY

  • High-Knowledge of your class/abilities/specs
  • Able/willing to fill different roles when asked
  • High-Knowledge of all bosses/encounters on the agenda
  • Ability to adapt to change quickly

    There is no hand-holding here. If a trial performs better than you, they will be brought in for progression. If for whatever reason we need to bring in a PUG, and they perform better than you, you may be sat and we may potentially recruit them to fill your role. As harsh as this may sound, it is necessary to compete at the level we intend to.
